Batlah

Batlah is a Saudi Arabian luxury perfume house. Its Shopify Plus storefront pairs a bespoke, brand-forward design with high-performance e-commerce, giving fragrance discovery the same craft as the scent itself.

Client Batlah
Role Project Manager
Market Saudi Arabia
Date 2024
Live store batlah.com →

My Role

Supporting a luxury fragrance launch

I manage the ongoing Shopify Plus support retainer for Batlah, coordinating releases and storefront updates so the site keeps pace with new fragrance drops and seasonal campaigns.

The Brief

Luxury branding, built to convert

Batlah needed a bespoke design focused on luxury and branding without sacrificing performance. The storefront leans into flavor-profile storytelling for each fragrance, guiding shoppers from scent notes to checkout.

Result

A scent house that performs as well as it looks

The finished store balances mobile-responsive, conversion-focused UX with the visual richness a luxury perfume brand demands, and stays that way through ongoing support.
