Nayomi

Nayomi is a Middle Eastern lingerie retailer headquartered in Dubai. This Shopify Plus launch blends elevated brand storytelling with careful user experience, performance-first development, and a mobile-ready storefront built for GCC shoppers.

Nayomi needed a digital storefront that reflected the brand’s premium lingerie identity while delivering fast, reliable performance across GCC devices. The project required a tight launch cadence, strong cross-team coordination, and a smooth transition from design to Shopify Plus delivery.

I led the project rhythm for the Nayomi launch, translating business goals into a clear delivery plan. I aligned stakeholders, managed timelines, and kept design, development, QA, and support teams moving in sync.

Elegance and timelessness drive the Nayomi visual system. We refined the user experience around product discovery, funnel clarity, and checkout ease, while keeping the interface clean and emotionally confident.

The development phase focused on performance, speed and efficiency so the site could support a high-volume GCC audience without losing the luxury feel.

Since shoppers in the region rely heavily on mobile, the build prioritized responsive navigation, quick load times, and streamlined product browsing. I reviewed mobile usability feedback and ensured the final release met real customer behavior.

Nayomi launched as a polished Shopify Plus store with a refined customer journey and strong ongoing support readiness. The new site reflects a premium lingerie brand while keeping operations and updates easy for the client.
