La Vie en Rose

La Vie en Rose is a Canadian lingerie and loungewear retailer headquartered in Montreal. Creative971 built the brand's Shopify Plus expansion into Saudi Arabia, localizing the global identity for Arabic-speaking shoppers while keeping the original HQ build in view.

Client La Vie en Rose Inc.
Role Project Manager
Market Canada (HQ) — Saudi Arabia (KSA)
Date 2023
Live store lavieenrose.com.sa →

My Role

Managing a global brand's expansion into KSA

I manage the Shopify Plus support retainer for La Vie en Rose's Saudi Arabia storefront, keeping the localized site running while staying aligned with the global HQ build in Canada.

The Brief

One brand, two markets

La Vie en Rose needed its global identity preserved while expanding into Saudi Arabia — full Arabic localization, streamlined checkout, and mobile-first design built for a new region.

Result

A Canadian brand, ready for the Gulf

The result is a Shopify Plus storefront that carries La Vie en Rose's global design language into Saudi Arabia, with ongoing support keeping both markets running smoothly.
