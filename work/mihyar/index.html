Project & Support UAE Shopify Plus

Mihyar

Mihyar is a luxury menswear brand specializing in traditional Arabic clothing. This Shopify Plus build pairs clear lines and minimalism with a lightweight, performance-first codebase, giving the brand a storefront as considered as the garments it sells.

Client Jamjoom Fashion
Role Junior Project Manager
Market UAE — GCC
Date 2025
Live store mihyar.com →

My Role

Keeping the Shopify Plus build on schedule

As Junior Project Manager on the Creative971 Shopify team, I coordinated between design, development and QA to keep the Mihyar build moving on schedule, translating brand requirements into a clear delivery plan and flagging risks early.

The Challenge

An elevated identity for a heritage menswear brand

Mihyar needed a digital experience that felt as premium as its traditional Arabic menswear, without slowing shoppers down. Clear lines and minimalism guided every decision, keeping the interface out of the way of the product.

The brief called for a state-of-the-art Shopify Plus solution with a lightweight codebase, built to stay fast as the catalogue and traffic grew.

Design & Delivery

Lightweight, fast, and built to last

The UI/UX was designed for quality and timelessness rather than trend, matching the enduring character of the brand's traditional clothing. Underneath, the Shopify Plus codebase stayed lightweight, optimized specifically for speed and performance.

Mobile-First Product Experience

Multi-buy discounts and precise sizing

Regional customer behaviour made mobile-first design a priority from day one. Product pages were built around multi-buy discounts and clear size measurement guidance, with a simplified navigation that kept the purchase path short.

Result

A refined storefront ready for GCC shoppers

Mihyar launched as a fast, minimal Shopify Plus store that reflects the brand's traditional Arabic menswear heritage while giving GCC shoppers a smooth, mobile-ready path from browsing to checkout.
