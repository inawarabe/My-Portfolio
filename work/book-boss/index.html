Project UAE

Book Boss

Book Boss is a Dubai-based online-only bookstore offering next-day shipping. Its Shopify storefront launched in 2025 with a clean, comfortable design built to make browsing a growing catalog simple.

Client Book Boss
Role Project Manager
Market UAE
Date 2025
Live store bookboss.ae →

My Role

Launching Dubai's next-day online bookstore

I managed the Shopify build for Book Boss, coordinating design and development through to its 2025 launch as a Dubai-based online-only bookstore.

The Brief

Clean design that lets the books do the talking

Book Boss wanted a comfortable, clutter-free storefront that highlighted next-day shipping and made browsing a growing catalog simple from day one.

Result

A fresh Shopify storefront, ready to grow

The finished store gives Book Boss a clean online home built for growth in the UAE market, with product clarity front and center.
